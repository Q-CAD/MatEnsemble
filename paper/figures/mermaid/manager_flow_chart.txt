flowchart TD
    A[Start FluxManager.run] --> B[Refresh free CPUs/GPUs]
    B --> C[Write status.json and logs]
    C --> D[Submit ready chores that fit]
    D --> E[Wait for completed Flux futures]
    E --> F[FutureProcessingStrategy.process_futures]
    F --> G{Chore succeeded?}
    G -- yes --> H[Mark complete]
    H --> I[Decrement dependent counts]
    I --> J[Move newly unblocked chores to ready queue]
    J --> K{Adaptive strategy?}
    K -- yes --> D
    K -- no --> L[Return to outer loop]
    G -- no --> M[Record failure]
    M --> N[Cascade dependency_failed to dependents]
    N --> L
    L --> O{Ready, running, or blocked chores remain?}
    O -- yes --> B
    O -- no --> P[Workflow complete]
